# SFIA Study Buddy

[View Live App](https://sfia-clarity-flow.base44.app)

SFIA Study Buddy (Skills Framework for the Information Age) is an AI-powered study assistant designed to help students learn, revise, and test their knowledge more effectively. It transforms study material into interactive learning experiences, making revision simpler and more engaging.

## Overview

SFIA Study Buddy is built to support students by combining AI-driven explanations, quizzes, and structured study support into one platform. It helps break down complex topics into easy-to-understand content and allows users to actively test their understanding.

## Features

* AI-powered study assistance for different subjects
* Interactive quiz generation from study material
* Simple explanations of complex topics
* Structured learning support for revision and exam preparation
* Clean and user-friendly interface for focused studying

## Use Cases

* Exam preparation and revision
* Understanding difficult concepts quickly
* Practicing with self-generated quizzes
* Daily study support and reinforcement learning

## Purpose

The goal of SFIA Study Buddy is to make studying more efficient by turning passive reading into active learning. It helps students retain information better through repetition, explanation, and practice.

## Tech Stack

Built using Lovable AI platform with integrated AI features for content generation and interaction.
